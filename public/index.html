Home

SpaceShapeShifter is a small shoot-em-up game made in HTML5, JavaScript and Canvas.

Instructions

Your goal is to survive as long as possible. You can collect diamonds in order to boost your ship's stats.

There are many possible strategies you can use. You can try to avoid as many enemies as possible, or face them head-on and blast their brains out. Your enemies will randomly leave diamonds after exploding which you can collect. The diamonds will gradually boost your ship's stats. Also, your ship can change form when you have at least 3 diamonds of the same color.

Forms

Your ship can transform in 3 forms:

  • Destruction form - increased damage. Requires red diamonds.
  • Speed form - increased speed. Requires green diamonds.
  • Defensive form - increased defense. Requires blue diamonds.

Keys

Arrows - move; Space - fire; Q - normal form; W, E, R - red(+damage), green(+speed), blue(+defense) form.
Refresh to restart.
